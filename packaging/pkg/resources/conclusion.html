Mac Doctor is installed.

Open Terminal and run:

mac-doctor
mac-doctor --fix
mac-doctor --html

Weekly scan: Mac Doctor will check your Mac every Sunday at 10 AM and send you a notification if anything needs attention.

To disable the weekly scan:

launchctl unload ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.macdoctor.scan.plist

To uninstall completely:

sudo rm /usr/local/bin/mac-doctor
rm ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.macdoctor.scan.plist
sudo rm -rf /usr/local/share/mac-doctor
All diagnostics run locally. No data is sent anywhere.
